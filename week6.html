Summary of Week 6

For Week 6 of the HPS program, the participants learned more about DFM principles relating to varying features along injection molded parts and how the thickness must remain consistent along the part. One important point which was made was to avoid having undercuts in the CAD design, since the presence of these details would make injection molding very difficult if not impossible. Differences in plastic solidifying throughout the formation of be part were made apparent with the idea of keeping in mind the injection molding aspects.
