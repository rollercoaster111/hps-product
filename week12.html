Week 12: HPS Presentations

"The success of your presentation will be judged not by the knowledge you send but by what the listener receives." -Lilly Walters

Week 12 Summary

For Week 12 of the HPS program, the HPS participants were able to finally display their work through presenting their projects to an audience. Typically this would be done in-person, but due to the nature of the program and the current state of the pandemic, the presentations were done virtually. Each participant had between 5-10 minutes to present, and this would be followed by any questions the audience had for them.
